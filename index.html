COE Global Engineering Campus

Welcome to the College of Engineering's Global Engineering Campus, the College of Engineering presence in the Global University System. For the purpose of these pages, the Global Engineering Campus concerns all Northeastern University campuses outside Massachusetts where the college expands, creates, and maintains educational and research programs.

From this page, you can explore the status of our programs, get to know our full-time faculty and staff, and the policy of our engineering campus, as well as links to the teams that support the global university system.

For any questions or further information, please contact the Associate Dean of the Global Engineering Campus and the Associate Director of Global Operations.
